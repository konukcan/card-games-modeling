Sorted_by_rank r1x LOCAL Level 1

Description: Read ranks left-to-right; they never go down (e.g., 5,7,7,9,J,…).

Lambda notation: λh. is_sorted(map(get_rank_val))
Rule: Sorted_by_rank (r1x) Lambda: λh. ... ──────────────────────────────────────── is_sorted (strict=False) └── map └── get_rank_val
Primitives used: is_sorted map get_rank_val
S_before_H r4x LOCAL Level 1

Description: Scan left-to-right; at least one ♠ is left of at least one ♥.

Lambda notation: λh. exists_ordered(get_suit, eq(SPADES), eq(HEARTS))
Rule: S_before_H (r4x) Lambda: λh. ... ──────────────────────────────────────── exists_ordered ├── get_suit ├── eq (suit1=SPADES) └── eq (suit2=HEARTS)
Primitives used: exists_ordered get_suit eq

Score_threshold_Rstar r6x SCORE Level 1

Description: Add ranks; add 10 if non-decreasing; add 6 if ≥3 ♥; win if total ≥ threshold.

Lambda notation: λh. gte(add(sum(map(get_rank_val)), if(is_sorted), if(gte(count_equal(get_suit)))))
Rule: Score_threshold_Rstar (r6x) Lambda: λh. ... ──────────────────────────────────────── gte (threshold=50) └── add ├── sum │ └── map │ └── get_rank_val ├── if (then_val=10, else_val=0) │ └── is_sorted └── if (then_val=6, else_val=0) └── gte (value=3) └── count_equal (value=HEARTS) └── get_suit
Primitives used: sum map get_rank_val is_sorted count_equal get_suit gte if add

Rule: Half_sum_diff_geN (r42x) Lambda: λh. ... ──────────────────────────────────────── gte ├── sub │ ├── sum │ │ └── left_half │ └── sum │ └── right_half └── length
Primitives used: halves sum map get_rank_val sub gte length
Half_sum_one_side_ge_2x_other r56x SCORE Level 2

Description: Split the hand into left and right halves; the sum of ranks in one half is at least double the other.

Lambda notation: λh. or(gte(sum(left_half), mul(sum(right_half))), gte(sum(right_half), mul(sum(left_half))))
Rule: Half_sum_one_side_ge_2x_other (r56x) Lambda: λh. ... ──────────────────────────────────────── or ├── gte │ ├── sum │ │ └── left_half │ └── mul (factor=2) │ └── sum │ └── right_half └── gte ├── sum │ └── right_half └── mul (factor=2) └── sum └── left_half
Primitives used: halves sum map get_rank_val mul gte or
